## About This Book

Imagine you could see the hidden architecture of human meaning-making‚Äîthe deep patterns that connect a Balinese water temple to a medieval cathedral, a Lakota Sun Dance to a Buddhist meditation retreat, an ancient mystery cult to a modern conspiracy theory.
